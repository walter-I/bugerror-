Risk disclaimer

This tool places real automated trades on Deriv synthetic indices using your own account credentials. These instruments are highly volatile and you can lose your entire stake on a single trade. Automated strategies — particularly martingale-style stake recovery — can deplete an account very quickly during a losing streak. Nothing in this app is financial advice. Only connect an account you are prepared to risk fully, and test on a demo/virtual account before using real funds.
